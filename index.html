What is ForecastOS?

ForecastOS is a universal forecasting dashboard built for traders, students, investors, and anyone who needs to make decisions based on data. It combines live crypto market data with a powerful forecasting engine that can analyze any time-series data you upload.

How It Works

1. Market Data: Live prices, market cap, volume, and fear/greed index from CoinGecko and Alternative.me APIs.

2. Forecast Engine: Upload any CSV with time-series data. Choose from Monte Carlo, Linear Regression, or Exponential Smoothing.

3. Plain English: Results are explained in human language, not jargon.

Data Sources

• CoinGecko: Prices, market cap, volume, 24h change for 17,000+ coins
• Alternative.me: Crypto Fear & Greed Index
• Your Data: Any CSV with a header row
